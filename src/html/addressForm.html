Because Old Dogs Rule ODR Signup

Please enter for each dog you are running in the ODR class. Qualifying dogs are any dog unable to participate on your regular team due to age or physical impediment. Dogs completing 6 of the 7 stages will receive a special certificate. There is no fee to participate. No dog left behind!

To complete a stage, each dog must log at least one mile total and share a photo to our page. Scores will be reported on a simplified scoresheet.
